<p class="header_box" id="description">Description</p>
<p>In 2003ï¼ŒLiu and Zhang used the SELEX  methodology to select RNA aptamers against bovine thrombin. These aptamers, T705 and T710, specifically recognize bovine thrombin and inhibit bovine thrombin-catalyzed fibrin clot formation<sup>[<a href="#ref1" style="color:#520049">1</a>]</sup>.<br></p>


<p class="header_box" id="SELEX">SELEX</p>
<p>In 2003, Liu and colleagues used SELEX. Starting from a pool of DNA templates, after transcription, aptamers that bind specifically to bovine thrombin were selected after 7 rounds of selection and amplification<sup>[<a href="#ref1" style="color:#520049">1</a>]</sup>.<p>
